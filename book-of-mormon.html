Introduction to the Book of Mormon's Authorship

The Book of Mormon is claimed to be a translation of ancient records by multiple prophets. Stylometric studies suggest distinct author styles, supporting this claim.

Stylometric Analysis

Analyses show unique "wordprints" for different prophets, such as Nephi and Alma, indicating multiple authorship.
