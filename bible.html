Introduction to the Bible's Authorship

The Bible is a collection of texts written by multiple authors over centuries. Stylometry confirms this by highlighting stylistic differences across its books.

Stylometric Analysis

Key differences in word frequency, sentence length, and vocabulary richness reveal distinct author styles. For example, Paul's letters differ significantly from the Gospel of John.
