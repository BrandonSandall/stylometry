What is Stylometry?

Stylometry is the statistical analysis of literary style. It examines features like word frequency, sentence structure, and vocabulary to identify unique author styles.

Common Techniques

  • Word Frequency Analysis
  • Sentence Length and Structure
  • N-grams
  • Vocabulary Richness

These techniques help determine whether a text was written by one or multiple authors.
